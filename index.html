About

I am a passionate lifelong gamer and Game Programmer specialized in Unity, with over 9 years of professional experience in the mobile games industry (iOS & Android). Throughout my career, I have built and optimized games from prototype to launch, consistently delivering engaging, high-performance experiences.

I care deeply about clean, scalable code and sustainable development practices. I design systems with SOLID principles, composition over inheritance, and clear boundaries between gameplay, UI, and data layers. This approach keeps features modular and testable, reduces technical debt, and accelerates iteration for live-ops.

  • Maintainability: clear architecture, dependency inversion, and small, cohesive components.
  • Scalability: data-driven systems, Addressables, and continuous optimization efforts based on profiling insights, enabling stable performance as content scales.
  • Quality: code reviews, automated builds, and analytics-informed rollouts for safe releases.
  • Dependency Injection: leveraging Zenject to decouple systems, improve modularity, and enable easier testing and scalability through clear contract-based architecture.
